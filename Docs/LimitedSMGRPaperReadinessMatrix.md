## Paper Decision Rule

Use the highest paper section whose required receipts are actually inhabited:

1. W3-only paper only after accepted W3 authority.
2. W3 plus W4/W5 cross-band Drell-Yan paper only after accepted DY convention,
   W4 adequacy, and W5 correction or typed obstruction.
3. Limited known-limit SM/QFT + GR paper only after Candidate256 calibration,
   W4 matter/stress-energy, discrete Einstein law, and GRQFT validation receipts.

If any row is request-only, template-only, diagnostic-only, constructorless, or
blocked by missing authority, the corresponding claim remains forbidden.
