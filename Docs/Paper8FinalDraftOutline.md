# The Standard Model Matter Content from Heegner Discriminant Geometry

Date: `2026-05-30`
Status: final-draft outline; candidate/derived labels explicit; non-promoting

## Abstract

This paper presents the Heegner-discriminant route to Standard Model matter
content as a labelled derivation/candidate ledger.  The derived claims are
restricted to the discrete receipt-backed outputs: generation count,
prime-lane assignment, charge-quantisation structure, and the recorded
quark/lepton split.  The candidate claims remain labelled as candidates:
full gauge-group reconstruction, exact Yukawa values, CKM completion, anomaly
authority, and physical Standard Model promotion.

## 1. Claim Governance

The introduction should make the label system part of the theorem statement.

- `Derived`: receipt-backed finite/discrete arithmetic content.
- `Candidate`: geometrically motivated but not yet externally or formally
  promoted.
- `Open`: requires new proof, authority, or empirical calibration.
- `False promotion`: any claim that DASHI has already derived the exact
  physical Standard Model.
