# Common Notation Glossary for Papers 1, 3, and 8

Date: `2026-06-09`
Status: live-draft notation only

This glossary normalizes notation that is actually used in the current live
drafts. It is not a legacy-wide symbol dump.

| Symbol | Meaning | Used in |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `u` | Navier-Stokes velocity field | Paper 1 |
| `Delta_j u` | Littlewood-Paley shell projection of `u` at dyadic index `j` | Paper 1 |
| `E_{>K}(t)` | high-frequency tail energy above shell cutoff `K` | Paper 1 |
| `D_{>K}(t)` | high-frequency tail dissipation above shell cutoff `K` | Paper 1 |
| `F_{>K}(t)` | nonlinear tail-flux term in the fixed-shell identity | Paper 1 |
| `theta(K,t)` | tail-flux to dissipation ratio `|F_{>K}|/D_{>K}` | Paper 1 |
| `K_*` | danger shell selected from the blowup or tail-flux scenario | Paper 1 |
| `A1, A2, A4, A5, A6, A7, A8, A9` | named assertions in the Navier-Stokes reduction ladder | Paper 1 |
| `r_4, r_6, r_7, r_8, r_9` | ordered radii used in the support and closure ladder | Paper 1 |
| `eps_4, eps_6, eps_7, eps_8, eps_9` | ordered smallness parameters used in the compatibility budget | Paper 1 |
| `H_N` | finite carrier Yang-Mills Hamiltonian or transfer operator at depth or size `N` | Paper 3 |
| `m_N` | finite spectral gap for `H_N` above the vacuum sector | Paper 3 |
| `m_*` | uniform finite spectral margin, when available | Paper 3 |
| `m_latt(beta)` | fixed-lattice mass-gap lower bound at coupling `beta` | Paper 3 |
| `beta` | lattice inverse coupling parameter | Paper 3 |
| `V/null` | quotient of a pre-inner-product space by its null space | Paper 8 |
| `ClosurePipeline` | canonical Paper 8 grammar taking finite algebraic data to inner-product, quotient, completion, and consumer stages | Paper 8 |
| `U-1a` | Paper 8 core closure statement for the canonical closure grammar | Paper 8 |
| `U-1a-H` | per-lane justification surface attached to `U-1a` | Paper 8 |
| `JvN` | Jordan-von Neumann inner-product recovery step | Paper 8 |
