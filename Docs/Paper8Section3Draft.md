hypothesis.  It is not a derivation of the Standard Model matter
representations.

absolute value.  The carrier assigns the first three discriminants

```text
gcd(3,4) = gcd(3,7) = gcd(4,7) = 1.
```

exhaustion.  The assignment is recorded as a structural hypothesis: after the
three pairwise-coprime quark lanes are filled, positions four through six

```text
vol(X0(4)) / vol(X0(8)) = 1/2
```

```text

vol(X0(4)) / vol(X0(12)) = 1/4.
```
