## Operational Q3 geometric series

`DASHI.Foundations.OperationalQ3GeometricSeries` gives an executable rational core for

```text
s_n = (3^n - 1) / 2
```

and proves

```text
2 s_n + 1 = 3^n
s_n + 1/2 = 3^n / 2.
```

The residual is therefore exactly a `3^n` factor times the 3-adic unit `1/2`, recording the increasing precision behind

```text
1 + 3 + 9 + ... -> -1/2
```

in the 3-adic completion. A reusable completed `Q3` field remains an explicit completion authority.
