# PNF, operational IR, memory and learning

This tranche makes the PNF/IR division explicit:

- extractors propose span-anchored candidate structures;
- PNF enumerates, invalidates, compares, composes and revises semantic possibilities;
- only an admissible and resolved PNF may project into a domain IR;
- a domain IR is semantically coarser but operationally sharper;
- execution receipts record the rule, query, action or refusal produced from the IR.

## Stable pipeline

```text
raw text/audio
-> spaCy/Stanza/ASR/rules/sidecar semantic parsers
-> CandidatePNF
-> AdmissiblePNF
-> ResolvedPNF
-> DomainIR
-> IRExecutionReceipt
```

No extractor or sidecar semantic representation may bypass the PNF gates and directly promote a world fact or operational decision.

## Versioned memory

Memory stores the full EventPNF plus valuation, salience, confidence, phase, retrieval policy, action bias and provenance. Revaluation, habituation, reinforcement, extinction and phase realignment preserve the remembered event and alter its internal learning state. Extinction does not erase the historical association.

Belief nesting is PNF-valued and depth-indexed. Common belief is explicitly bounded and compressed rather than promoted as exact infinite common knowledge.

## Braid transport

Memory, expectation, desire, avoidance, action and observation are separate PNF lanes. Transport order may be coherent, quotient-equivalent, residually different or incompatible. The canonical regression records a noncommuting residual rather than forcing equality.

## Multilingual and speech boundary

ASR alternatives remain a lattice of candidate PNFs with timings and confidence. Translation is a transport with preserved invariants and residuals such as lost evidentiality, aspect ambiguity or non-coextensive legal terminology. UD, UCCA, MRS and AMR are sidecar producers, not canonical PNF authorities.

## NASHI

Text/news PNF joins the market lane only through source-binding, event-time, revision, entity-resolution, no-lookahead, mechanism and regime receipts. Textual evidence alone never upgrades the independently validated mechanism level.
