This index maps the current inhabited receipts, explicit false-promotion

Flag-state vocabulary:

- `inhabited`: the named receipt/identifier exists as the cited bounded surface.
- `true local flag`: the cited receipt records a bounded local success only.
- `false promotion`: the cited flag or lemma keeps the stronger claim blocked.
- `frontier/open`: the cited receipt name is a frontier slot or obligation
  whose closing convergence theorem is not yet inhabited in this index.
- `authority boundary`: external literature or governance authority is recorded,
  but not imported as a completed DASHI theorem.

Authority vocabulary:

- `internal receipt`: the row is governed by a DASHI receipt rather than an
  external source authority.
- `CitationAuthority`: the row cites a source but does not claim a frozen
  machine-readable artifact.
- `ArtifactAuthority`: the row binds a machine-readable artifact/checksum.
- `mixed authority`: the row combines internal receipts with explicit external
  citation or artifact boundaries; the citation use must preserve that split.
