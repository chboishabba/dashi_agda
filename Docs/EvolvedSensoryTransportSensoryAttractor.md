# Evolved Sensory Transport And Sensory Attractor Classes

This receipt adds a non-promoting formal surface for generalized sensory
transport:

1. world physics
2. physical transport
3. receptor chemistry
4. neural encoding
5. connectome/body propagation
6. perceptual/behavior quotient
7. evolutionary retention

## Agda Surfaces

- `DASHI.Physics.Closure.SensoryAttractorClasses` defines the reusable
  transport, low-dimensional attractor, evolved-attractor, and non-promotion
  certificate records.
- `DASHI.Biology.EvolvedSensoryTransport` packages those records as a
  biology-facing receipt with population, development, ecology, measurement,
  and retention hooks.

## Claim Boundary

The modules explicitly block:

- exact qualia recovery;
- arbitrary cross-sense identity;
- theorem-level biology claims without external receipts;
- latent state injection from quotient readouts;
- evolutionary optimality claims without measurement support.

The allowed claim is narrower: biological sensory systems may be represented
as typed lossy transports into low-dimensional attractor/quotient classes,
with evolutionary retention modeled only through supplied criteria and
measurement receipts.
