This implementation joins the repository's existing balanced-ternary, zero-fibre,
fibre-braid, belief-update, ultrametric, supervoxel, observer-manifold, and
Calabi–Yau authority surfaces without promoting a clinical or neurological model.

## Formal spine

1. `ConstraintClopenRefinement` separates intensional constraint inclusion from
   extensional state-region inclusion. A fine specification extends a coarse one,
   while its admissible region is contained in the coarse region.
2. `CoarseMetricPushforward` records metrics, probabilities, and statistics as
   projection- and aggregation-indexed observables over fine fibres.
3. `ReflexivePresentationFamily` gives one hidden psychological carrier binary,
   balanced-ternary, unbalanced-ternary, n-ary, continuous-code, wave-phase, and
   full-fibre presentations.

   attractors, predictive anti-targets, negative-valence attractors, and genuine

5. `AdaptedInterpretationBoundary` separates present-information action from
   retrospective trajectory interpretation and requires an explicit correction
   residual between them.
6. `ReflexivePsychologicalDynamics` supplies the belief → expectation → action →
   world → observation → belief loop and a finite bank-run regression.
7. `BayesianReflexiveDynamicsBridge` makes evidence provenance and prior action
   explicit before Bayesian scoring.
8. `ClopenPsychologicalSupervoxel` treats public balanced zero as a coarse clopen
   constraint region with distinct fine dynamical subvoxels.
9. `ThreeAdicDigitAttractor` closes the finite prefix-contraction surface without
   postulates and makes Q₃ completion / minus-one-half identification an explicit
   authority interface.
10. `CalabiYau369CompactFibreBridge` records the guarded dimensional reading

## Boundaries

- No clinical diagnosis or treatment claim is promoted.
- No claim is made that brains literally implement Itô or Stratonovich SDEs.
- No claim is made that Base369 proves string compactification.
- No Q₃ completion or rational `-1/2` theorem is fabricated without a supplied
  completion authority.
- Continuous and statistical outputs remain coarse observables whose fibres may
  contain distinct hidden states and transition laws.
