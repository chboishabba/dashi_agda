# Paper 6 Submission Readiness Review

Paper 6 is not ready as a derivation paper. It is ready to be shaped as a
diagnostic/conjecture manuscript if the introduction and abstract are explicit
that the carrier formulas are fail-closed numerical diagnostics, not physical
CKM promotions.

## Positive Results

- `lambda = alpha1*pi*sqrt(3)` gives `lambda ~= 0.224403`, about `0.056%`
  low against a PDG-sized comparison value `0.22453`.
- The geometric-resummation diagnostic for the `p3-p5` lane gives
  `A ~= 0.825598`, about `0.049%` low against `A = 0.826`.
- The `p2-p5` bridge entry has a clear arithmetic surface:
  `Y13_bridge = 18/sqrt(24) * 8*pi/3 = 24*pi/sqrt(6)`.
- Multiplying the bridge entry by the golden ratio gives a close low-`Vub`
  diagnostic, but it remains an observed correction, not a carrier derivation.
- The Hecke-character phase at `p = 29` gives a concrete CP-phase diagnostic,
  `delta_29 ~= 1.0035 rad`.

## Negative Results

- The requested formula
  `alpha1^3 * phi * 14/(1-alpha1/2) * (8*pi/3)/sqrt(24)` gives
  `|Vub| ~= 0.002774`, about `24.8%` below `0.00369`. It should not be
  described as a `0.16%` match.
- The simpler `bridge * phi` formula gives `|Vub| ~= 0.003493`, about `5.3%`
  below `0.00369` and about `2.1%` below the low comparison target `0.00357`.
- The corrected Jarlskog diagnostic from the requested `phi*14` convention is
  about `0.709` times a PDG-sized target, still not a physical derivation.
- The full physical Yukawa eigenbasis and absolute Yukawa normalisation are not
  yet certified.

## Required Intro Disclaimer

Suggested wording:

> This paper reports fail-closed CKM diagnostics generated by the DASHI
> Heegner-lane carrier. The formulas for `lambda` and the geometric diagnostic
> for `A` are numerical carrier readbacks with small comparison residuals. The
> golden-ratio correction in the `1-3` lane and the Hecke-character phase are
> recorded as diagnostic hypotheses, not as derivations of the physical CKM
> matrix. In particular, `physicalCKMPromoted`, `physicalVubPromoted`, and
> `goldenRatioCarrierDerived` remain false in the Agda receipt index.

## Verdict

Submission path: diagnostic/conjecture paper only. The manuscript can be
scientifically useful if it foregrounds the clean `lambda` and `A` diagnostics,
then states the `phi`, `Vub`, phase, Jarlskog, and physical-eigenbasis gaps as
the research program.
