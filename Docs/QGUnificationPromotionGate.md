1. **Discretization correctness** — controlled continuum limit, grid independence, and scaling law over a refinement tower.

4. **Measurement and prediction** — physical and quantum measurement maps, repeated-trial statistics, refinement-stable predictions, a falsifiable observable, and empirical matching.
