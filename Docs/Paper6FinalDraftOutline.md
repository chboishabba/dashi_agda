# Arithmetic Derivation of Wolfenstein Parameters from Heegner CM Geometry

Date: `2026-05-30`
Status: final-draft outline; diagnostic; non-promoting

## Abstract

This paper presents the Heegner-CM arithmetic route to the Wolfenstein
hierarchy as a diagnostic derivation rather than a completed CKM theorem.  The
strong claims are limited to the recorded arithmetic estimates for `lambda`
and the `Vcb`/`A` lane: `lambda` is sub-percent accurate against the target
Cabibbo value, and the `Vcb`-scale diagnostic is sub-percent at the current
frontier after the two-loop/deg23 route is recorded.  The open claims are kept
explicit: `Vub`, the CP phase, Jarlskog authority, and the full physical CKM
matrix are not derived.

## 1. Claim Boundary

The draft opens with the exact claim discipline.

- Derived/diagnostic: Heegner CM geometry supplies a concrete arithmetic
  construction of the Wolfenstein hierarchy surface.
- Strong comparison: `lambda` and `Vcb` are reported at sub-percent diagnostic
  accuracy under the stated conventions.
- Open: `Vub`, CP violation, Jarlskog normalization, full CKM diagonalisation,
  PDG authority binding, and physical Yukawa normalization.
- Forbidden wording: no physical CKM promotion, no Standard Model completion,
  and no terminal phenomenological claim.
