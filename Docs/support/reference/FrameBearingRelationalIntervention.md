# Frame-bearing metacognition, relational compression, and intervention

## Governing separation

This extension formalises the synthesis

```text
distress as situated signal
+ knowledge as relational compression
+ politics as intervention in material feedback loops
```

without making any lane exclusive. The invariant is:

```text
experience != interpretation != causal explanation != action mandate
```

## 1.0 to 1.1 as a second-order state

The power-up is not represented as `1.1 * x` or a measured ten-percent gain.
It is represented as an experience-preserving frame-bearing state:

```text
x -> (x, frame, provenance, residual, stance)
```

The binary stance coordinate distinguishes immersion in a frame from observation
of that frame. This reuses the binary factor exposed by the `1,2,3,6,9`
hierarchy without claiming that six intrinsically means metacognition.

A frame-aware state does not prove its own causal interpretation, mandate an
action, or guarantee symptom relief.

## Plural situated hypotheses

A distress, anomaly, or gluing-defect signal opens a hypothesis field containing
personal, biological, relational, environmental, institutional, economic,
interpretive, and measurement possibilities.

Situated access may provide an epistemic advantage when it includes:

- access to lived conditions;
- access to the dominant governing frame;
- cross-frame comparison;
- critical method.

Position alone is not encoded as infallibility or complete authority.

## Proof-carrying relational compression

A local compression carries four non-discardable components:

```text
compressed model
provenance
retained remainder
overlap/gluing receipt
```

The module gives one finite two-context gluing example and reuses the temporal
QIT-application boundary. A local story is not promoted to universal truth or
community authority merely because it compresses and glues in one finite case.

## Material feedback interventions

An intervention receipt records:

```text
target
mechanism
expected effect
possible side effects
measurement
falsifier
rollback
provenance
```

The supplied advertising-pressure example is encoded as a candidate mechanism,
not as an adjudicated causal or historical result. Observing an association does
not prove a unique mechanism, and success in one case does not establish
universal generalisation.

## Observer constructions

Two observer extensions are separated.

### Exceptional observer

```text
X + {star}
```

This represents an overflow, exceptional marker, or rechart point. At the
finite arithmetic level:

```text
9 + 1 = 10.
```

### Reflexive observer

```text
X * C2
```

This attaches an immersed/frame-aware stance to every state. For a nine-state
carrier:

```text
9 * 2 = 18.
```

The two constructions are complementary, not identical.

## Evidence-gated recharting

Frame awareness alone does not imply chart failure. The exact finite decision
surface distinguishes:

```text
stable evidence                -> retain chart
refinable residual             -> refine within chart
gluing defect                  -> successor chart
MDL improvement                -> successor chart
```

The aggregate checks that a gluing-defect receipt at chart `10` permits chart
`11`, while an ordinary refinable residual remains at chart `10`.

Generic score inequalities, policy, governance, and the claim that a particular
defect requires immediate recharting remain producer obligations.

## 196883 plus one

The exact arithmetic remains:

```text
196883 + 1 = 196884.
```

The `+1` may be read in a named DASHI chart as a trivial, identity, or observer
summand. That interpretation does not prove:

- that `196883` is a dynamical period;
- that `196884` is a `JFixedPoint`;
- Monster representation authority;
- modular `j` authority;
- an automatic topology change.

## Integrated recursive route

The integrated pipeline is:

```text
experience
-> frame-bearing state
-> plural hypotheses
-> relational compression
-> validated intervention
-> observed outcome
-> residual and revision/rechart
```

This ensures that lived experience is preserved, the frame becomes inspectable,
causal explanations remain plural until tested, and outcomes feed back into the
next model rather than being retrofitted as proof of the initial theory.
