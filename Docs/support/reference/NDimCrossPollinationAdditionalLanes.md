- triadic/p-adic hierarchical address;

- standard-part observation.


finite coordinates rather than discarding infinitesimal differences.

## Respect, Connect, Reflect, Direct

`DASHI.Culture.RelationalProcessProtocolBoundary` packages a provenance-bounded
workflow analogy:

1. Respect: authority, consent, access, and boundary conditions.
2. Connect: relations among people, place, time, evidence, and obligations.
3. Reflect: overlap comparison, uncertainty, contention, and gluing defects.
4. Direct: bounded authorised action with dissent and review retained.

The workflow may guide ITIR design, but it is not claimed to implement or
replace Indigenous Knowledge. Reciprocity accounting is only one audit view and
does not exhaust the ethical relation.
