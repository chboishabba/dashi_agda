# 5. The Vub and CP-Phase Obstructions

The preceding sections isolate the arithmetic content that is presently
effective: the Cabibbo lane and the \(V_{cb}\)-scale lane have concrete carrier
formulae with meaningful numerical comparisons.  The same is not true for the
\((1,3)\) entry or for CP violation.  Both must remain open in the present
version of the paper.

The most direct extension of the \(V_{cb}\) carrier rule to the \((1,3)\)
sector is numerically false.  If the inert \(T_5\) contribution in
\(\mathbb{Q}(\sqrt{7})\) is read as the \((1,3)\) carrier degree, then

\[
  \deg_{13}=6
\]

and the corresponding carrier estimate gives

\[
  |V_{ub}|_{\mathrm{carrier}} = 0.02467 .
\]

Against the PDG comparison value

\[
  |V_{ub}|_{\mathrm{PDG}} = 0.00369 ,
\]

this is wrong by a factor of about \(7\).  Equivalently,

\[
  \frac{0.02467-0.00369}{0.00369} \simeq 5.69 ,
\]

so the relative error is approximately \(569\%\).  This is not a small
normalisation residual.  It is a structural failure of the naive extrapolation
from the \(V_{cb}\) lane to the \(V_{ub}\) lane.

The conclusion is therefore sharp.  The \(V_{cb}\) formula does not extend to
\(V_{ub}\) by the substitution \(\deg_{23}\mapsto\deg_{13}\) under the present
carrier assignment.  Either the \((1,3)\) mixing entry is controlled by a
different modular structure, or the generation indexing used by the carrier
lanes is not uniform across the \(2\)-\(3\) and \(1\)-\(3\) sectors.  In either
case, the present carrier formula does not derive \(|V_{ub}|\).

The CP phase is also not derived.  The current carrier CKM entries used in the
real arithmetic diagnostics are real, and no accepted source of a physical
complex phase has been identified.  The complex CM period

\[
  \tau_D = e^{i\pi/f_D}
\]

is only a candidate source of phase data.  At present it is not connected to
the observed CKM phase \(\delta_{\mathrm{CP}}\), nor is there a derivation that
transports this period into a physical Jarlskog-normalised CKM matrix.

Thus Section 5 contributes a negative theorem-level diagnostic rather than a
new positive prediction.  The \(V_{ub}\) carrier extrapolation fails by an
order-one factor, and the CP phase remains an open arithmetic and representation
problem.  No physical \(V_{ub}\), CP-phase, Jarlskog, or full CKM promotion is
asserted here.
