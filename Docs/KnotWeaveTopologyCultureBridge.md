# Knot Weave Topology Culture Bridge

`DASHI.Culture.KnotWeaveTopologyCultureBridge` records knot, weave, and braid
language as bounded semantic/topological operator carriers for the culture lane.

The safe reading is:

```text
knot / weave / braid carrier
  -> bounded topological role
  -> bounded semantic culture role
  -> inverse culture operator surface
  -> non-promoting observation boundary
```

The bridge is theorem-thin.  It only gives typed carriers and canonical guards
for local binding, repeated interlace, and ordered crossing motifs.  It does
not prove that cultures share universal meanings, that topological similarity
is identity, that qualia are recovered, or that the lane has authority to
promote cultural claims.

The blocked claims are cultural universals, identity or qualia closure,
authority promotion, and context-free equivalence.
