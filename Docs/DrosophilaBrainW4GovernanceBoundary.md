## Allowed Lateral Claims

Drosophila evidence may count as lateral W4 support only in these forms:

- provenance/checksum support for a named public Drosophila data route;
- candidate biological-data support for a future W4 chemistry-law or
  Candidate256 request surface;
- non-promoting sanity evidence that a biological artifact was selected,
  checksummed, and summarized reproducibly;
- negative evidence that the selected hemibrain coarse-grain runs do not
  establish multiscale persistence;
- review-queue evidence after a direct W4 lane records typed exhaustion and no
  retarget availability.

Allowed phrasing:

- "candidate lateral support for W4 review";
- "checksum-bound Drosophila genome/CDS packet";
- "non-promoting Brain/Drosophila adjacent evidence";
- "possible input to a future W4 chemistry-law or Candidate256 provider
  request";
- "Brain lateral review may be considered only after typed W4 exhaustion
  without retarget availability."

## Forbidden Claims

Drosophila or Brain evidence must remain non-promoting when it is used to
claim, imply, or replace any of the following:

- W4 physical calibration;
- Candidate256 physical calibration authority;
- Candidate256 physical calibration external receipt;
- physical unit carrier, Nat-to-unit calibration map, or dimensional
  preservation law;
- matter-field or stress-energy construction from W4;
- biological recovery or full Drosophila neuroscience interpretation;
- multiscale persistence, given the current coarse-grain obstruction;
- Brain lane closure;
- physics lane promotion;
- unification.

Forbidden phrasing:

- "Drosophila closes W4";
- "Brain evidence promotes W4";
- "the genome packet is a calibration law";
- "hemibrain evidence recovers biology";
- "coarse-grain persistence is established";
- "Drosophila/Brain evidence supplies Candidate256 authority";
- "Brain/Drosophila evidence replaces a W4 receipt."

## Brain Lateral Review Trigger

Brain lateral review is triggered only when all of these are present:

1. A direct W4 lane has a typed exhaustion receipt for the relevant W4 route.
2. The same direct W4 surface records that no retarget remains available.
3. The Drosophila/Brain packet is checksum/provenance-bound and names its
   candidate scope.
4. The proposed review states the exact W4 consumer it might support.
5. The proposal preserves the no-promotion, no-unification, and
   no-biological-recovery boundaries.

If any condition is absent, the status is `not eligible for Brain lateral
review`.
