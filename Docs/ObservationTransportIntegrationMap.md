# Observation Transport Integration Map

## Pattern

The common DASHI pattern is:

```text
latent carrier
  -> lossy observation quotient
  -> admissibility gate
  -> transport operator
  -> promotion boundary
```

This is now named locally in:

```text
DASHI.Interop.ObservationTransportSpine
```

The module is a vocabulary surface, not a validation result. It exists to keep
the cross-domain joins typed without collapsing observation, causation,
subjective experience, or global ontology.

## Quotient Discipline

Every observation map is treated as many-to-one unless separately proven
otherwise:

```text
pi(sigma1) = pi(sigma2)
does not imply
sigma1 = sigma2
```

This is why the brain lane can safely say joint observations may narrow a
latent equivalence class, while still refusing state recovery:

```text
(pi_fMRI, pi_percept, pi_behavior)
  -> smaller [sigma]
  != recovered sigma
```

## Non-Claims

The integration map does not claim:

- fMRI is the percept;
- behavior recovers qualia;
- correlation proves causation;
- a quotient is invertible;
- a local receipt is a global ontology theorem;
- SeaMeInIt artifacts validate a live manufacturing run without stage receipts.
- a cached light-transport closure is globally exact `O(1)` physics;
- a video reuse/hash quotient is admissible without equality or correction
  evidence and counted side information.
- a protein/hormone candidate observation proves protein folding, disease
  causation, cell-fate prediction, hormone-to-behaviour closure, or completed
  chemistry-to-biology derivation.
- an animal utterance, posture, or behavioural motif gives direct access to
  subjective experience, full cell biology, disease causation, intent, or a
  species-universal dictionary.
- a Wikidata/Wikipedia join packet is edit authority, truth authority, or a
  complete meaning space.
- a machine/program motif surface is an optimal compiler, hardware closure, or
  semantic-equivalence proof.
- bidirectional transport is a universal translator or a zero-defect
  isomorphism without a witness.
- evolutionary motif persistence proves abiogenesis, origin-of-life closure, or
  biology inevitability.
- multi-observer fusion proves universal determinism, hidden variables,
  peer-review infallibility, or full latent recovery.
- observer-local branch navigation proves metaphysical free will, recovers
  intent, or enumerates all branches.
- culture, reading, art, politics, or WAIS-style quotient probes provide
  clinical diagnosis, aesthetic truth, or development closure.
- cross-domain operator equivalence is global ontology, substance identity,
  untyped exact equivalence, or universal reducibility.
- metaphor/simile alignment guarantees perfect understanding, qualia recovery,
  universal exact translation, or context-free optimal communication.
- knot/weave/braid motifs prove cultural universals, identity closure, qualia
  closure, or cultural authority.
- fibre pressure bounds alone calibrate a domain threshold, prove metric
  completeness, construct a global optimizer, or promote a transport claim.

Positive evidence may refine a candidate surface, but severe or negative gates
remain dominant at promotion boundaries.
