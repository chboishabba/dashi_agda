## Required Non-Circular Route

WC3-controlled small-field expansion must prove `m_eff(a) > 0`; asymptotic freedom may then supply physical scale behavior. This is blocked until the non-circular route is proved.

## Blocked Clay Requirements

### blockedWightmanDistributions
- Requirement: Construct Wightman distributions for the four-dimensional theory.
- Evidence: OS/Wightman authority and boundary receipts are present, but no complete distribution-level reconstruction proof is present in Sprint 91-103.
- Reason: Partial OS material is not a completed Wightman reconstruction.
- Required resolution: Complete OS-to-Wightman reconstruction for the candidate Schwinger functions.

### blockedPoincareCovariance
- Requirement: Prove Poincare covariance of the reconstructed Wightman theory.
- Evidence: Euclidean and transfer-boundary receipts exist, but the Minkowski covariance theorem is not closed.
- Reason: Euclidean-invariance evidence alone does not establish Lorentzian covariance.
- Required resolution: Derive Poincare covariance after reconstruction and bind it to the candidate field algebra.

### blockedSpectrumCondition
- Requirement: Prove the positive-energy spectrum condition.
- Evidence: Transfer-gap and OS boundary material exists, but the Wightman spectrum theorem is not closed.
- Reason: A transfer-gap receipt is not itself the spectrum condition.
- Required resolution: Prove the energy-momentum spectrum lies in the closed forward cone and bind the gap to that spectrum.

### blockedAllCompactSimpleG
- Requirement: Prove the theorem for any compact simple gauge group G.
- Evidence: The current manuscript lane is SU(3)-scoped.
- Reason: The Jaffe-Witten problem is not restricted to SU(3).
- Required resolution: Generalize group-dependent estimates and nontriviality witnesses to arbitrary compact simple G.

### blockedNonCircularMassGapProof
- Requirement: Prove the mass gap without assuming the physical mass scale.
- Evidence: Sprint 103 records a continuum bridge using WC3, RG effective mass, transfer comparison, and asymptotic freedom.
- Reason: Sprint 104 cannot certify that the route avoids circular dependence on the desired gap.
- Required resolution: Prove WC3-controlled small-field expansion gives m_eff(a) > 0, then use asymptotic freedom for physical scale behavior.
